About GALI MOVE

Your trusted partner for visa applications, financial services, and vehicle rentals in Rwanda.

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Kigali, GALI MOVE has grown from a small consultancy to a leading service provider for travel and financial solutions in Rwanda.

We specialize in helping individuals and businesses navigate complex visa processes, particularly for Mauritius, while offering comprehensive support for banking services and vehicle rentals.

  • Expert guidance on central banking procedures
  • Competitive foreign exchange rates
  • Reliable vehicle rental options
